— Why Meridian works

Two molecules. Each chosen for one job. Together they rewrite your skin's instructions.

01 · Wrinkle depth
Bakuchiol upregulates collagen genes
0.5% bakuchiol activates the same collagen-I and collagen-III pathways retinol does — but skips the inflammatory cascade that causes peeling. Measurable line reduction by week 4.
02 · Pigmentation
Vitamin C disrupts tyrosinase
12% L-ascorbic acid blocks the enzyme that produces melanin clusters, fading existing pigmentation and stopping new hyperpigmentation from forming. Stabilized with ferulic acid and vitamin E.
03 · Tolerability
Zero peeling, zero down-regulation
Unlike retinol, you don't need to ramp up. You don't need a "purge week." You don't need to skip nights. The Duo works with the skin's barrier instead of stripping it.
04 · UV-safe
Sun-stable. Use morning or night.
Bakuchiol is photostable, unlike retinol. Vitamin C in this form is actually photoprotective. Use either or both molecules in your AM ritual without worrying about sun sensitivity.
05 · Clean
11 ingredients across both bottles
No fragrance. No silicones. No filler oils. No alcohol. No essential oils. Vegan, gluten-free, manufactured in a CGMP-certified facility in California. Third-party tested for purity.
06 · Minimal ritual
Two steps, twice a day. Done.
AM: 4 drops vitamin C serum after cleansing. PM: 3 drops bakuchiol oil as your last step. No layering 12 things. No spreadsheet of acids. A ritual you'll actually keep.
